ARIJIT SINGH Live Concert

Nov 23 Lal Bahadur Shastri Stadium, Hyderabad

My Music My Country,” a nice platform that presents gifted Indian artists to perform and play the music that's home to our country. My Music My Country initiated by Hyderabad Talkies aims to re-introduce the ethnicity of Indian Music and brings to you melodies of all kinds.

 We are glad to announce Arijit Singh live in concert for the first time ever in Hyderabad. The man with a voice of magic and melody in his soul. One of the best singing sensations ever in Bollywood. He also has to his credit some super hit songs of Tollywood and other film industries, 132+ Songs, 55+ Awards in the music world and with a huge fan base all over the world. Hyderabad Talkies brings to you the voice of love and pain in association with Telangana Tourism & BMS live.  

 Hyderabad Talkies is an accomplished event management company who’ve created a landmark in the field of live events by successfully conducting the maestro AR Rahman’s live in concert to celebrate his spirit over 25 glorious years in Indian cinema in 2017  

 The event will take place on 23rd November 2019 in Hyderabad and we look forward to welcoming all the Arijit Singh fans and music lovers across South India on 23rd November 2019.
